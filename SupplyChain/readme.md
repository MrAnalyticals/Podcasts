
This report outlines how **artificial intelligence** is revolutionising global supply chains to overcome modern challenges like **geopolitical instability** and **climate volatility**. The text identifies a strategic shift from reactive troubleshooting to **predictive and autonomous operations**, driven by four major industry trends. Key pillars of this transformation include establishing **end-to-end visibility**, using **predictive analytics** for demand forecasting, and implementing **adaptive execution** through robotics and AI agents. Real-world case studies from companies like **MSC**, **Dow**, and **Venchi** demonstrate how unifying data on cloud platforms leads to significant **cost savings** and **operational efficiency**. Ultimately, the sources argue that integrating AI is no longer optional but essential for building **resilient**, **sustainable**, and **competitive** supply networks.
